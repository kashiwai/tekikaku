FAQ

About the Account

Q. I forgot my password. What should I do?
Please reset your password from "Forgot Your Password?" on the login screen.
Q. I haven't received my member registration email.
Please reconfirm whether it was automatically sorted into the spam folder or it is not set to receive mails from the "{%SITE_DOMAIN%}" domain.
Q. I want to cancel the membership. What should I do?
You can cancel the membership from "Procedures to Cancel the Membership" on My Page. * You need to log in.

All account information once canceled will be deleted and cannot be restored.
In addition, your owned "{%CURRENCY_1%}" and "{%CURRENCY_3%}" will also be deleted. Please consider it before proceeding to cancel the membership.

About the Play

Q. Are there any restrictions on the operating environment for playing {%SITE_TITLE%}?
Please confirm them at "Recommended Specs for {%SITE_TITLE%}" of "For the First Time Users".
{%SITE_TITLE%} does not work properly on IE and Edge.
If a malfunction occurs, please use the browser listed above and confirm the operation.
If you still have troubles when using the browser listed above, please contacting us by specifying your operating system and the browser.
Q. Can I play {%SITE_TITLE%} on my smartphone?
You can play {%SITE_TITLE%} on both iPhone and Android with the compliant browsers.
* For the compliant browsers, please confirm "Recommended Specs for {%SITE_TITLE%}" of "For the First Time Users".
If you play without Wi-Fi, be careful of packet usage.
Q. What happens if the business hours end during the Play?
As soon as the round you are playing at the end of business hours is over, it will be automatically settled and you will be left.
Q. There is no sound. What should I do?
Turn on the voice button in the upper right of the play screen.
Adjust the volume with the speaker on the terminal.
* Since the sound source is taken directly from the housing, sound cracking may occur.
Q. The image quality has dropped (or the image has stopped) during the Play. What should I do?
Please reboot your browser.
You can continue to use your {%CURRENCY_2%}, etc.
* However, it will be automatically settled after a certain period of time without resuming the Play.
Q. How can I avoid the lag (delay) of the button operation?
Since the games are operated by displaying the actual pachinko / slot machines via the internet, the lag (delay) may occur due to the line conditions and terminal specifications. Please enjoy the Play on understanding this.
Q. What happens to my play data if the internet connection is lost during the Play?
If it is within 3 minutes from the disconnection, you can resume from the {%CURRENCY_2%} you had before the disconnection.
If the internet connection is interrupted during the Play and it is difficult to reconnect for some reasons, the {%CURRENCY_2%} held before the disconnection will be automatically settled after a certain period of time.
